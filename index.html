How to play

It's Halloween and evil Zombies and Monsters rose from the dead. Collect bombs and try throwing them at your enemies before they're able to kill you.
You can also collect candy. It's Halloween after all.
Be warned: a mighty laser shooting monster has been spotted. Throwing multiple bombs at it might be helpful.
Good luck adventurer and happy Trick or Treating!
