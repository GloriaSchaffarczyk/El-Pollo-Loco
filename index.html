How to play

As midnight descends on Halloween, the graveyard is roamed by evil zombies and monsters. 🧟 Arm yourself with bombs 💣 and throw them at your enemies to protect yourself and your loved ones.
Don't forget to snatch up some candy 🍬 it's Halloween, after all! But be aware: a mighty laser-shooting monster has been spotted. 👀 Throwing multiple bombs at it might be helpful. And now: Good luck adventurer and happy trick-or-treating! 👻🎃
